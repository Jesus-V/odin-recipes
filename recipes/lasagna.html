Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish that's beloved worldwide for its layers of rich flavors and comforting warmth. At its core, lasagna consists of alternating layers of wide, flat pasta sheets, a savory tomato-based sauce, creamy b√©chamel sauce, and a variety of cheeses, typically mozzarella, Parmesan, and sometimes ricotta. These layers are often interspersed with cooked ground meat such as beef, pork, or sausage, as well as vegetables like onions, garlic, mushrooms, and spinach.

The pasta sheets are usually pre-cooked before assembly to ensure they are tender and fully cooked by the time the dish is baked. Each layer is carefully arranged in a baking dish, creating a hearty and satisfying casserole. The top layer is often finished with a generous sprinkling of cheese, creating a golden, bubbly crust when baked.

Ingredients

  • Meat: 1/2 pound of ground pork and 1/2 pound of lean ground beef
  • Onion: 1 diced onion cooked with meat until translucent
  • Canned Tomatoes: 1 can of tomato sauce and 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • Fresh Herbs: 2 Tablespoons of parsley(chopped) and 1 garlic clove(crushed)
  • Sugar: Just a pinch to balance the acidity from the tomatoes
  • Spices and Seasonings: Dried Basil, Dried Oregano, salt, and black pepper
  • Noodles: Oven-ready Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese: cottage cheese, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan
  • Eggs: Used as a binding agent and to make the cheese layers extra creamy!

Steps

  1. Cook the Meat:Cook the ground meat in a skillet until browned and crumbly. Add the onion and continue cooking until it's translucent. Stir in the canned tomato products, half of the parsley, garlic, basil, 1.5 teaspoons of salt, oregano, and sugar
  2. Cook the noodles: Boil the lasagna noodles in lightly salted water until they're al dente
  3. Make the cheese layer: Mix cottage cheese, Parmesan cheese, eggs, the remaining parsley, the remaining salt, and pepper in a bowl
  4. Assemble the lasagna: Layer the ingredients according to the recipe (starting with sauce and ending with mozzarella) until the lasagna is assembled
  5. Bake the lasagna: Cover with foil and bake in the preheated (375 F) oven for about half an hour. Remove the foil and continue baking until the top is golden brown (5-10 mins)
